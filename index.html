About Me

Short bio · Breve biografía

I am a computer scientist and Professor at the University of Camagüey, Cuba, with a PhD in Technical Sciences (2021), an MSc in Computer Science (2013), and a BSc in Computer Science (2010). My work lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence, soft computing, evolutionary computation, and applied optimization, with a strong focus on real-world decision-making problems.

Over the last years, I have contributed to research on estimation of distribution algorithms, prototype-based learning, decision-support systems, and data-driven models for complex domains such as smart grids, energy systems, and biomedical applications. I actively collaborate with international teams and participate in scientific competitions and benchmarking initiatives in evolutionary computation and machine learning.

Soy profesor de la Universidad de Camagüey, Cuba, y doctor en Ciencias Técnicas (2021), con máster (2013) y licenciatura (2010) en Ciencias de la Computación. Mi trabajo se centra en la inteligencia artificial, el softcomputing, los algoritmos evolutivos y la optimización aplicada a problemas reales de toma de decisiones.

En los últimos años he trabajado en algoritmos de estimación de distribuciones, aprendizaje basado en prototipos, sistemas de apoyo a la decisión y modelos basados en datos para redes inteligentes, sistemas energéticos y aplicaciones biomédicas, entre otros. Colaboro de forma activa con grupos de investigación internacionales y participo en competiciones y benchmarks científicos en computación evolutiva y aprendizaje automático.

Projects / Proyectos

Selected research and applied work
Evolutionary Computation for Smart Grids and Energy Systems

Design and application of estimation of distribution algorithms and other evolutionary approaches to solve complex energy resource management problems under uncertainty, including participation in international competitions and benchmarks.

Data-driven Models for Environmental and Biomedical Domains

Development of machine learning and soft computing models for toxicity prediction, chemoinformatics, and biomedical data analysis, with an emphasis on interpretability and robust generalization.

Decision-Support Systems and Educational Tools

Construction of expert systems and decision-support tools for teaching and learning (e.g., linear algebra) and for supporting decision making in different applied domains.
